Nightmare Securites LLC


Founded in 1702, Nightmare Securities LLC is a company run for monsters, by monsters. We pride ourselves on diversity. Our staff boasts a wide variety of evil, from several Hantu Bungku, to an ancient Vampire Queen, to a moderately sized dragon. No matter your mythical insurance needs, Nightmare Securities LLC is here for you.


Our Owners

Sylvius Alabaster Von Schlitz celebrated his 809th birthday last December. He is tired. So very tired. Sometimes we wheel him out to sit by the window so he can look upon the rolling green hills of his youth. Other times we just bring him a stiff whiskey and hope he will not call down the wrath of the Old Gods upon our tender heads. He has been in the insurance buisness since its inception.


Eviline Augustus Throop is a unicorn. No, actually. She doesn't speak, but she stares at us with baleful eyes and upon occasion, gores a traveler. She vomits gold in our shoes from time to time. Though she hasn't expressed any direct intrest in financing the company, we're pretty sure it's what she wants. She's been hanging about for years. We've tried everything to make her leave. She won't. So we figure, what's the harm in allowing her to give back to the community?


Slithik is a creature of few words, but many thoughts. It has no gender, or age. We think it is behind some of our more creative products? Though it's hard to confirm this for certain. Some mornings we just wake up and there's something in the lab that wasn't there before. Often covered in the same thick purple slime that Slithik's tentacles exude.
